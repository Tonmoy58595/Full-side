How I Waste Time

Being a CSE student comes with many challenges, but it's easy to waste time. Here are some common time-wasting habits.

1. Uncontrolled Social Media Use

Spending hours scrolling through Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, or YouTube. Engaging in unnecessary debates or watching memes.

2. Gaming Addiction

Spending too much time playing online games like FREE FIRE, Valorant, or CS:GO, while neglecting studies and project work.

3. Enrolling in Unnecessary Courses

Enrolling in trendy courses like AI, Blockchain, or Cybersecurity, but not completing them or applying the knowledge in real projects.

4. "Planning" Without Action

Thinking of new project ideas, but never starting them. Procrastinating and saying "I will start tomorrow."

5. Watching Irrelevant Videos

Spending time watching Netflix, anime, or tech videos on YouTube without a clear purpose or learning goal.

6. Fake Productivity

Spending hours organizing notes, buying new planners, or trying new productivity tools without focusing on actual work.

7. Group Study with Too Much Gossip

Spending study time chatting with friends, discussing irrelevant topics instead of studying important subjects.

8. Overthinking Career Options Without Taking Action

Creating freelancing profiles, thinking about startup ideas, but never taking action to learn the necessary skills.
